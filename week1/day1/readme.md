# CRM Basics - Salesforce

## 1. What is CRM?
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. 
It is a system that helps businesses manage 
interactions with customers and potential customers.

## 2. Why Companies Use Salesforce
- It organizes customer data in one place
- Tracks sales and opportunities
- Improves team communication
- Automates business processes
- Used by thousands of companies worldwide

## 3. Salesforce Key Objects

### Account
A company or organization you do business with.

### Contact
A person associated with an Account.

### Opportunity
A potential sale or deal being tracked.

## 4. Real-World Mapping (Travel Example)

I chose a Travel System to explain Salesforce objects.
Lead
This is someone who just searched for flights or hotels online but didn't book anything yet. They are just looking around.
Contact
This is the actual traveler who signed up and gave their details like name, email and phone number.
Account
This is the travel agency or maybe a company that is booking trips for their employees.
Opportunity
This is when the traveler is seriously about to book a trip. Like they picked dates, destination and are ready to pay.
